Golf is Great

Even If the Golfer... Isn't!

Golf is Challenging, Even for the Best of the Best!

For an amateur, golf can be incredibly frustrating, and also fantastically rewarding. There have been plenty of rounds I've wanted to quit halfway through, but finish with moments that remind me just how much fun the sport can be! But...

Even the Best Golfers in the World Struggle Sometimes!

Greg Norman Blows Six Shot Lead at 1996 Masters
  • Snap hook into the trees from the opening tee
  • Double-bogey on 12 after coming up short in the water

'My distance control was off. At Augusta, if you are not putting the ball within a four-foot radius of where you want to be, then you are going to run into trouble.'
-Greg Norman, Winner of Zero Majors

Phil Mickelson Falls Apart on 18 at 2006 US Open
  • Needed par to win, bogey to force playoff
  • Here's how the last hole went for Phil:
    • Sliced Drive
    • Second shot into a tree
    • Third into a greenside bunker
    • Fourth shot runs all the way through the green
    • Misses chip for a 5 to force playoff

'This one hurts more than any tournament because I had it won. It hurts because I had it in my grasp and just let it go.'
-Phil Mickelson: Incredible Shotmaker, Very Sore Loser

Rory McIlroy Can't Handle The Pressure of Three Days Atop The Leaderboard
  • 65-69-70 through first three rounds, four shot lead, all alone in 1st place
  • Mistakes all over on Sunday, cards a final round 80, ties for 15th place

'There are a lot worse things that can happen in your life. Shooting a bad score in the last round of a golf tournament is nothing in comparison to what other people go through..'
-Rory McIlroy, Number 1 Ranked Golfer in the World

Golf With Friends

Golfing with friends is truly the best way to play. The conversations had on a course can be used as a way to deepen relationships... or talk smack about your buddy's slice off the tee. My wife (pictured above) picked up golf a few years back, originally as a way to spend time with me on a shared hobby. Soon, she found herself hooked. Meeting new people, joking around, and who could forget the ladies golf outfits!
